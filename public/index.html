What is SCinet?

SCinet is the world's fastest and most robust network during the annual SC conference. It delivers a dedicated, high-capacity network infrastructure meticulously crafted by volunteer experts from various sectors, including industry, academia, and government. Each year, SCinet assembles and maintains this network using equipment, software, and services generously contributed by leaders in the industry.

Our mission?

To continuously advance the frontiers of networking technologies and innovations by uniting the most talented and brilliant minds from research and education networks, research universities, and the industry in the US and around the world.

What is the value of contributing to SCinet?

SC24 stands as the premier global gathering for high-performance computing professionals, with SCinet serving as a vital platform for contributors to showcase their equipment, software, services, and expertise. This collaboration fosters synergy across industry, academia, and government in designing, testing, and operating advanced technologies crucial for data-intensive scientific endeavors. With over 200 dedicated volunteers from more than 100 diverse organizations, SCinet is the heart of the SC experience, providing hands-on access to and experimentation with these extraordinary technologies.
